The Role of the Opposite Hitter

The opposite hitter plays a key offensive role, primarily attacking from the right side of the court. They are essential to both offense and defense. In addition to attacking, they are often tasked with blocking and passing duties.

Key Responsibilities

  • Play a key role in attacking from the right side of the court.
  • Provide both offensive and defensive support during the match.
  • Block opponent’s attacks, particularly from outside hitters.
  • Assist in setting plays when necessary.

Skills Needed

  • Strong hitting skills with the ability to attack from a variety of angles.
  • Good defensive capabilities, including blocking and passing.
  • Quick decision-making ability during fast-paced rallies.

Tips for Aspiring Opposite Hitters

  • Focus on improving both your offensive and defensive skills.
  • Work on timing and communication with the setter to enhance attack efficiency.
  • Practice blocking and positioning to effectively counter opponent’s outside hitters.
